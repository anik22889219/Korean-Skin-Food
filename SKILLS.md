## 📈 Marketing & Growth Skills
*Managed via SkillKit*

### Strategy & Ideas
- **marketing-ideas:** Brainstorm growth tactics, strategies, and promotion ideas.
- **content-strategy:** Plan content pillars, blog topics, and editorial calendars.
- **competitor-profiling:** Research and analyze competitors directly from their URLs.
- **competitors:** Create competitor comparison and "alternative to" landing pages.
- **launch:** Plan product launches, feature announcements, and GTM strategy.

### Conversion & Copy
- **copywriting:** Write persuasive headlines, benefit-focused body copy, and CTAs.
- **copy-editing:** Polish, tighten, and refresh existing marketing copy.
- **cro:** Optimize pages and forms for higher conversion rates using data.
- **marketing-psychology:** Apply behavioral science and cognitive biases to marketing.
- **popups:** Design high-converting exit-intent modals and announcement banners.
- **signup:** Optimize registration, trial activation, and account creation flows.
- **onboarding:** Improve post-signup activation and reduce time-to-value.

### Channels
- **ads:** Strategic planning and optimization for Google, Meta, and LinkedIn ads.
- **ad-creative:** Generate and iterate on ad copy variations (RSA, Facebook Ads) at scale.
- **emails:** Build automated lifecycle sequences, drips, and nurture flows.
- **cold-email:** Write B2B outreach sequences that get replies and handle follow-ups.
- **social:** Create, schedule, and optimize content for LinkedIn, X/Twitter, and more.
- **video:** Produce video content and scripts using AI avatars or code (Remotion).

### SEO & Organic
- **seo-audit:** Diagnose and fix technical, on-page, and indexing SEO issues.
- **ai-seo:** Optimize for AI search engines (AI Overviews) and LLM citations.
- **programmatic-seo:** Scale content using data-driven templates for long-tail keywords.
- **schema:** Implement structured data (JSON-LD) for rich snippets in Google.
- **aso:** Optimize App Store and Google Play listings for visibility and downloads.
- **site-architecture:** Map sitemaps, information architecture, and internal linking.
- **directory-submissions:** Submit to startup, SaaS, and AI directories for discovery.

### Retention & Revenue
- **pricing:** Make informed decisions on pricing tiers, packaging, and monetization.
- **paywalls:** Optimize in-app upgrade screens, upsell modals, and feature gates.
- **churn-prevention:** Build systems to retain users and recover failed payments.
- **referrals:** Create viral loops, affiliate programs, and ambassador strategies.
- **revops:** Manage lead lifecycle, CRM automation, and sales-marketing handoff.
- **sales-enablement:** Create pitch decks, one-pagers, and sales collateral for closing.

### Measurement & Tools
- **analytics:** Set up and audit conversion tracking (GA4, GTM, Event Tracking).
- **ab-testing:** Design, implement, and analyze A/B tests and split experiments.
- **customer-research:** Analyze voice-of-customer, interviews, and review mining.
- **free-tools:** Plan and build interactive lead-gen tools (calculators, generators).
- **image:** Generate and optimize marketing images, mockups, and social assets.

## 🚀 Usage Instructions
To use any of these skills, simply ask Gemini CLI to load them:
- **Core Skills:** `skillkit read <skill-name>`
- **Marketing Skills:** `skillkit read <skill-name>`

Example: `"Use the cro skill to analyze our homepage."`
